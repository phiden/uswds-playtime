My favorite m&m shortbread

This cookie's been in my life since I was a little kid -- my Girl Scout leader used to make it for us to take camping. It whips up super quick and is perfect with an afternoon cup of coffee (or tea!).

  • Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup m&ms, chocolate chips, or your favorite added treat

  • Directions

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  • Mix all ingredients using a hand mixer to create dough.
  • Press dough into an 11x13" baking pan, and bake for 20 - 30 minutes, or until the top is golden-brown.
  • Let cool, slice into squares, and try not to eat them all at once!

Buffalo dip

I usually make this game-day deliciousness with shredded chicken, but one of my besties is vegetarian so I've discovered it's also perfect with white beans instead of chicken. Tofu might work too!

  • Ingredients

  • 1 cup Ranch dressing
  • 16oz cream cheese (Neufchatel works also)
  • 1 small bottle (5oz) of Cholula or your favorite hot sauce
  • 1/2 rotisserie chicken, shredded, OR a can of white beans
  • 1 cup of cheddar cheese, shredded

  • Directions

  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  • Mix Ranch, cream cheese, and Cholula until incorporated -- this is quickly done with a hand or stand mixer.
  • Add shredded chicken or your beans and mix.
  • Pour mixture into a glass baking pan and top with shredded cheese.
  • Bake for 15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  • Serve with your favorite tortilla chips
